History of AFRDI

The Air Force Research Institute (AFRDI) was established in 1950 with a mission to advance aerospace technologies and support national defense. Over the decades, AFRI has been at the forefront of numerous breakthroughs, from pioneering jet propulsion systems to developing cutting-edge materials for spacecraft.
